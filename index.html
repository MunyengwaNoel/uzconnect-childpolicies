Safety Standards & Contact

UZConnect — Published Safety Standards (CSAE)

This page constitutes UZConnect's externally published standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including reporting channels, enforcement, and contact information. It is intended to meet app store child safety standards for social/dating apps.

Report a Safety Concern

1) Our Commitment (Zero Tolerance)

UZConnect strictly prohibits any content or behavior that sexualizes, exploits, harms, or endangers children or young people. We remove such content, disable accounts, and cooperate with lawful investigations.

Prohibited on UZConnect

  • Any child sexual abuse material (CSAM/CSAE), including images, videos, text, audio, links, or AI-generated content that depicts or sexualizes minors.
  • Sexual solicitation, grooming, sextortion, trafficking, or attempts to facilitate exploitation of minors.
  • Age-misrepresentation to interact with minors in sexual contexts; sharing or requesting sexualized depictions of minors.
  • Accounts, groups, or messages that coordinate, advertise, or normalize CSAE in any form.

2) Safety by Design

  • Age gates and policy-driven account restrictions for underage users.
  • Report, block, and mute controls available on profile and message screens.
  • Automated and human moderation for suspected CSAE signals, with priority queues.
  • Link/domain and media safeguards; repeat-offender device/account blocks.

3) Enforcement & Reporting to Authorities

  • Immediate removal of CSAM/CSAE and permanent account termination for confirmed violations.
  • Preservation of evidence consistent with applicable law; cooperation with competent authorities subject to legal process.
  • Escalation playbooks for imminent harm, grooming, and sextortion scenarios.

4) How to Report

If you encounter content or behavior that may involve CSAE, please report it through one of the channels below. We prioritize these reports 24/7.

In‑App Reporting

Use the Report button on a profile, post, or message to send details to our Safety Team.

Path: Profile ⋯ → Report → CSAE / Exploitation

Email (Safety Desk)

Write to noel@uzconnect.co.zw with links, screenshots, and context.

We prioritize: subject line starting with [CSAE].

Law Enforcement & Legal

For lawful requests and preservation notices, contact noel@uzconnect.co.zw.

We will verify requests and respond in line with applicable law and our data policies.

Emergency / Imminent Harm

If a child is in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services first.

After contacting authorities, notify noel@uzconnect.co.zw with “URGENT” in the subject.

5) Review, Appeals, and Transparency

  • Notices are sent to impacted users (unless legally prohibited) when enforcement occurs.
  • Appeals can be submitted via in‑app support or email to noel@uzconnect.co.zw.
  • We publish periodic summaries of CSAE enforcement in our transparency notes.

6) Data Handling

  • We minimize retention and restrict internal access to CSAE evidence to authorized personnel only.
  • We share information with competent authorities when legally required or to address imminent risk.

7) Education & Prevention

  • In‑app tips and safety prompts discourage risky interactions and explain reporting tools.
  • We may provide links to trusted child protection resources in your region.
